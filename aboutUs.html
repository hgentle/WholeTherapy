Ryan Jones, LCSW, CCPT

I am deeply committed to your wellbeing, and to supporting you as you heal and as you reconnect with yourself, life and others.

I am a NARM Master Therapist and use EMDR and NARM interventions, both highly effective and modern, science-based therapies that will help you overcome trauma and adverse childhood experiences (ACEs) to move forward with the life and relationships you want.

Also, I support a compassionate and structured approach to divorce through Collaborative Practice, where a team of legal, financial, and mental health professionals come together to support clients to navigate divorce amicably and without litigation. As a mental health coach in collaborative practice, I support healthy co-parenting, resolution of conflicts and the establishment of clear boundaries and direction for relationships.
